Why being a Data Architect?

It is crucial that data is accessible and reusable for society and industry. Nowadays, it is essential that data can be accessed and repurposed not only by professionals but also by regular users, such as those using social media and trying to download their own information. Tasks such as creating, saving, ingesting, transforming, processing, and visualizing data require time and effort. Additionally, maintaining security, executing administration, performing operations, detailing orchestration, preserving good software engineering practices, and defining data architecture strategy are all critical aspects of the data ecosystem....
